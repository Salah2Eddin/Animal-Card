Polar Bear

Polar bears skin is black , although its transparent because thier white fur hides it.

  • Scintific Name: Ursus maritimus
  • Avarage Length: 2.4–3 meters
  • Avarage Weight: 350–700 kg
  • Habitat: the Arctic

Polar bears are marine mammals, and spend much of their time on Arctic sea ice.
Their fur is thicker than any other bears' and covers even their feet for warmth and traction on ice.
